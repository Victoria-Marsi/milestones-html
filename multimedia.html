This video demonstrates how to use the <iframe> tag. You can also use the <video> tag which is used to embed video content in a document, such as a movie clip or other video streams. The <video> tag contains one or more <source> tags with different video sources. The browser will choose the first source it supports. If your video is from youtube you can:

  1. Click the share option
  2. Click embed
  3. Copy and paste information into the body of the HTML file

The <audio> tag is used to embed sound content in a document, such as music or other audio streams. If your audio is from Spotify you can:

  1. Click the share option
  2. Click copy embed code
  3. Paste information into the body of the HTML file
